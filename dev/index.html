Give part-time employees the Opportunity to Work

Measure E will require businesses to offer any available additional hours to their current, qualified part-time employees before hiring more workers.

Measure E will help hourly workers get access to enough hours so their paycheck covers the bills and puts food on the table.

San Jose has a crisis of underemployment. To avoid paying fair wages and benefits, too many employers hire new part-time workers instead of giving hours to current employees.

Measure E will ensure that if a company has more hours of work and an existing part-time employee who is qualified to do the job, that employee gets the opportunity to work those hours, so they can earn enough to make ends meet.
